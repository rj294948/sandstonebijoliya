Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Rajasthan SandStone Hub, we collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and contact details when you register an account or contact us.
  • Business Information: Company name, GST number, business address for factory registrations.
  • Product Information: Stone details, images, prices, and specifications when you list products.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our stone marketplace services
  • To process transactions and facilitate stone purchases
  • To connect buyers with authentic stone manufacturers
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To communicate with you about updates, offers, and support
  • To ensure compliance with our terms and policies

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With stone manufacturers when you express interest in their products
  • With service providers who assist in operating our website
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • In connection with business transfers or mergers

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data in a readable format

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Improve our services and user experience

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
